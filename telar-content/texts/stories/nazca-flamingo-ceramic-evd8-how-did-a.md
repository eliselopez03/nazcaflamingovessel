---
title: "How did a ceramicist come to decorate this vessel with flamingos?"
---

**As seen in figure 2, present populations of Andean and Puna Flamingo are primarily concentrated in the highlands of Chile, Bolivia, and Argentina. But, there have been some spotted occasionally into Southern Peru. Though the flamingo and Nazca homelands do not coincide and it is very unlikely for a ceramicist to have encountered this bird, it is not completely unrealistic that a Nazca traveler or guest interacted with a flamingo in their lifetime.**
