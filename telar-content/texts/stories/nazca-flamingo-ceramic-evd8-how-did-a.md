As shown in Figure 2, the current populations of Andean and Puna Flamingos are primarily concentrated in the highlands of Chile, Bolivia, and Argentina. But there have been occasional spots in s some spotted ocouthern Peru. Though the flamingo and Nazca homelands do not coincide and it is very unlikely for a ceramicist to have encountered this bird, it is not completely unrealistic that a Nazca traveler or guest interacted with a flamingo in their lifetime.
