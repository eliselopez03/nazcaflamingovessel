---
title: "How did the Nazca utilize this vessel?"
---

Completed vessels were used in a wide range of contexts, such as feasts, ritual offerings, especially burials, and domestic activities. The enclosed top with its opening, combined with the detailed flamingo imagery wrapping the lower half, suggests this vessel wasn't really meant for everyday use. Like most Nasca polychrome ceramics, it probably lived more in ritual or burial contexts than in domestic ones.![Hand-built Pottery](https://eliselopez03.github.io/nazcaflamingovessel/iiif/objects/screenshot-20260602-at-24422-pm/screenshot-20260602-at-24422-pm.jpg)
